# Personas

> Phase: design | Project: gsp-app | Generated: 2026-03-19

---

## Dev — The Builder Who Ships Code, Not Product

- **Demographics:** Full-stack developer, 25-40, terminal is home
- **Goals on this site:** Understand what GSP does in 10 seconds. See proof it works (the site itself). Copy the install command and leave
- **Pain points:** Does not want marketing fluff. Suspicious of AI claims. Will inspect CSS and judge the site by its own quality
- **Usage context:** Arrives from a GitHub README, a Bluesky post, or an HN comment. On laptop, often in a split view next to terminal. Wants to evaluate fast, install faster
- **Success metric:** Copies `npm install` within 60 seconds of landing

## Des — The Designer Curious About Terminal Tools

- **Demographics:** Product designer, 28-42, Figma-native but curious about code-side workflows
- **Goals on this site:** Understand GSP's design methodology. See that it respects design process (dual diamond, strategy before visuals). Evaluate whether it fits into a modern workflow
- **Pain points:** Tired of tools that say "make it pretty" without process. Needs to see that GSP produces real design artifacts, not decorations
- **Usage context:** Arrives from design Twitter, a blog post, or a colleague's recommendation. On laptop or tablet. Will read more, scroll deeper, and evaluate the pipeline visualization carefully
- **Success metric:** Reads through the pipeline section and understands the dual-diamond methodology. Shares the link
