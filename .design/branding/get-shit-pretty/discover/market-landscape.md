> Phase: discover | Brand: get-shit-pretty | Generated: 2026-03-08

## Industry Context

GSP sits at the intersection of three converging markets: AI-assisted development tools, design systems/engineering, and CLI-first developer workflows. None of these markets alone describes what GSP does — GSP is creating a new category at their overlap.

## Market Size and Growth

**AI Coding Tools:** The AI coding assistant market reached $7.37 billion in 2025 (up from $4.91B in 2024) and is projected to hit $30.1 billion by 2032 at a 27.1% CAGR. Claude Code hit $1 billion ARR within six months of public launch — faster than ChatGPT's early trajectory. Cursor reached $500M ARR by end of 2025. GitHub Copilot holds 42% market share but is losing ground to newer entrants.

**Design Systems:** The global design system market is estimated at $1.2B in 2025, driven by enterprise adoption of tools like Figma, Storybook, and component libraries. shadcn/ui has become the de facto component library for Next.js projects.

**CLI Developer Tools:** Terminal-based AI workflows exploded in 2025-2026. Claude Code, OpenCode, Gemini CLI, and Codex CLI are the primary runtimes. GSD (Get Shit Done) pioneered spec-driven development in the terminal and is used by engineers at Amazon, Google, Shopify, and Webflow.

## The Vibe Coding Problem

In February 2025, Andrej Karpathy coined "vibe coding" — describing AI-driven development where developers describe intent and AI generates code. By early 2026, Y Combinator reported 25% of its Winter 2025 batch had codebases that were 95% AI-generated.

The problem: vibe coding produces functional code but not coherent products. Brand consistency becomes the casualty of speed. Design systems documentation exists, but no tool connects brand strategy to code generation in a structured pipeline. This is the gap GSP occupies.

## The Design Engineering Convergence

"Design engineer" is emerging as a recognized discipline. Job postings for the role have surged. The traditional handoff between designer and developer is dissolving — vibe coding tools let anyone prototype, but craft and coherence require structured thinking. GSP positions itself as the structured thinking layer.

Key signal: DevOps is no longer just backend engineering — it is a collaborative ecosystem where designers, developers, and design agencies work together. GSP rides this wave.

## Where the Industry Is Heading

1. **CLI-first AI workflows become dominant.** The terminal is the new IDE for AI-assisted development. Products built here need design coherence baked into the workflow.
2. **Brand coherence becomes the bottleneck.** AI makes building fast. So fast that the hard problem shifts from "can we build it" to "does it hold together as a product."
3. **Design systems move upstream.** Today, design systems document components. Tomorrow, they will prescribe brand strategy, verbal identity, and visual coherence before a single component is built. GSP is building this future.
4. **The "do nothing" alternative erodes.** Asking Claude to "make it look nice" produces inconsistent results across sessions. Developers will demand structured brand workflows the same way they demanded structured project management (GSD).
