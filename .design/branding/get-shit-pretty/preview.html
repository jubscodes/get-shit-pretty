Voice

A senior design engineer pairing with you. Direct, warm, craft-obsessed. Two modes: Warm Mentor (default) and Manifesto (key moments).

Precise

Every word earns its place. "Design engineering system" not "AI-powered design platform."
Clinical, robotic, or stripped of personality.

Irreverent

The name is Get Shit Pretty. We swear when it clarifies. Confidence, not cynicism.
Cruel, mocking, or trying to be edgy. Never at the user's expense.

Warm

We give a damn. We celebrate progress. One honest detail over a paragraph of encouragement.
Coddling, vague, or avoiding hard truths. Warm is "redo this, here's why."

Craft-forward

We talk about process, decisions, tradeoffs. We show the work. Making is the point.
Gatekeeping or implying "real designers" do it our way. Craft is inclusive.
