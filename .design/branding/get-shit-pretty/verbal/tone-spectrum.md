# Tone Spectrum

> Phase: verbal | Brand: get-shit-pretty | Generated: 2026-03-08

---

## Default Position

GSP's resting tone sits in the middle-casual range — structured enough to trust, casual enough to keep reading.

```
Formal       1 ─── 2 ─── [3.5] ─── 4 ─── 5  Casual
Serious      1 ─── 2 ─── [3] ─── 4 ─── 5    Playful
Authoritative 1 ─── 2 ─── [3] ─── 4 ─── 5   Friendly
Technical    1 ─── 2 ─── [3.5] ─── 4 ─── 5  Simple
Reserved     1 ─── 2 ─── [3] ─── 4 ─── 5    Enthusiastic
```

**Why these positions:**
- **3.5 casual:** The Creator archetype works in its own way. CLI-native means no corporate veneer, but structured craft isn't sloppy.
- **3 serious-playful:** The name is irreverent. The work isn't. We play at the edges, not the center.
- **3 authoritative-friendly:** Warm mentor — opinionated but never lecturing. Anthropic's warmth-meets-expertise.
- **3.5 technical-simple:** Audience speaks both languages. We use precise terms ("archetype," "prism," "positioning") but explain them in context.
- **3 reserved-enthusiastic:** Confident, not hype. We celebrate shipped work, not feature announcements. Vercel energy — the restraint is the flex.

## Tone Shifts by Context

| **README hero** | 2.5 | 2 | 2 | 3 | 2.5 | Manifesto |
| **README body** | 3 | 2.5 | 3 | 3.5 | 2.5 | Mentor |

| **Marketing site hero** | 2.5 | 2.5 | 2 | 3 | 3 | Manifesto |

| **Changelog** | 3.5 | 3 | 3 | 3.5 | 3 | Mentor |
| **Contributor guide** | 3 | 3 | 4 | 3 | 3.5 | Mentor |
| **Release announcement** | 3 | 3 | 2.5 | 3.5 | 3.5 | Manifesto |

### Context Notes

**Terminal output** stays terse and technical. The constraint is the medium — 80 characters wide, read mid-flow. No filler.

**Error messages** shift toward friendly. When something breaks, warmth matters more than wit. State what happened, state what to do.

**Social media** is the loosest GSP gets. Still precise, but the playfulness dial turns up. This is where irreverence breathes.

**Documentation** is the most structured. Serious, methodical, authoritative. The craft shows through thoroughness, not personality.

**Manifesto mode** applies to heroes, headlines, and first impressions. It pulls toward authoritative and reserved — declarative statements, no qualifiers.
