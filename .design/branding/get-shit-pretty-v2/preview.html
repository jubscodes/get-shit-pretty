Logo Direction — The Slash Mark
/gsp
The Slash Mark
The forward slash is GSP's native punctuation — every skill invocation begins with /gsp:. The invocation IS the identity. Two forms: /gsp for brand contexts, /gsp: for CLI contexts with the trailing colon.
/gsp: ◆◆
Inline CLI Mark
In terminal output, the brand mark includes state-aware diamonds. ◇ = pending, ◈ = active, ◆ = complete. The mark is alive — it reflects the state of your work.
Brand Archetype
Creator + Guide
The skilled friend who builds with precision and walks you through it
Creator: craft, precision, building things that endure. Empowers others to create — GSP doesn't design for you, it walks you through designing.

Guide: explains the why without making you feel lectured. Shows, doesn't just tell. Warm but direct — honest feedback that feels like help, not correction.
