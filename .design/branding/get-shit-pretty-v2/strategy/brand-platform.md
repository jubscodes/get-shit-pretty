# Brand Platform

> Phase: strategy | Brand: get-shit-pretty | Generated: 2026-03-19

---

## Purpose (Why GSP Exists)

AI made building fast. Nothing made building coherent.

GSP exists because speed without intention produces products that work but do not communicate. Builders ship features, not experiences. Colors are gut calls. Spacing is "whatever looks right." Typography is the first Google Font result. The result is software that functions but fails to earn trust, guide attention, or say anything about the people who made it.

GSP exists to make design thinking accessible where products are actually built -- inside the terminal, alongside the code, in the builder's native environment.

## Vision (Where GSP Is Going)

Every builder ships products that communicate as clearly as they function -- not because they hired a designer, but because design guidance was there when they built.

## Mission (What GSP Does)

GSP walks builders through the full brand-to-build journey inside the terminal -- from brand strategy to design tokens to component foundations -- so that design decisions are intentional, coherent, and owned by the people who ship the product.

## Values

### 1. Craft is a verb

Design quality is not a state you arrive at -- it is a practice you maintain. GSP treats every spacing decision, every color choice, every type scale as an act of building. Good enough ships; craft iterates.

**Behavioral test:** Does this output reflect care in the details? Would a designer look at this and know someone thought about it?

### 2. Walk alongside, never above

GSP guides by showing, not lecturing. Every recommendation comes with a reason. Every opinion has an escape hatch. The builder keeps ownership; GSP provides the thinking.

**Behavioral test:** Does this interaction make the user feel helped or corrected? Could they explain the *why* after reading it?

### 3. Terminal is home

The CLI is not a limitation or a fallback. It is the native environment for the people GSP serves. Every feature, every output, every brand moment must work in monospace on a dark background before it works anywhere else.

**Behavioral test:** Does this work in a terminal? If not, is the non-terminal version clearly secondary?

### 4. Opinions with reasons

GSP makes strong defaults because builders want guidance, not menus. But every opinion is grounded in design reasoning that the user can see, question, and override. Opinionated does not mean arbitrary.

**Behavioral test:** Can the user see *why* this choice was made? If they disagree, do they have enough context to make a different informed choice?

### 5. The brand is the proof

GSP is a design tool that designs itself. The quality of GSP's own identity, output, and interactions is the primary trust signal. If the tool's own brand is not coherent, nothing it produces will be trusted.

**Behavioral test:** Would someone seeing GSP's own design say "I want my product to look that considered"?

## Brand Promise

GSP makes design decisions visible and intentional, so that what you build communicates what you mean.

This is not a promise of beauty -- it is a promise of coherence. Pretty is the outcome when spacing, color, type, and hierarchy are working together with purpose. GSP promises that builders will understand *why* their design works, not just *that* it works.
